Write For Us

AgastyaThinkTank : Geopolitical and Economic Think Tank

Our readers are policymakers, business leaders and academics who are seeking to understand the complex forces shaping the world today.

If you are interested in writing for us, please submit a pitch to agastyathinktank@gmail.com Your pitch should include a brief overview of your proposed article, as well as your qualifications for writing on the topic.

We are particularly interested in articles that:

    Original insights into complex geopolitical, economic, or defense issues.
    Are well-researched and supported by evidence.
    Written in a clear and concise style.
    Relevant to our audience of policymakers, business leaders, and academics.

If your pitch is accepted, we will work with you to develop your article and ensure that it meets our high standards. We offer competitive pay and the opportunity to reach a global audience. We look forward to hearing from you!
